Welcome to the **AI-Native Systems Lab (ANSL)** at Sookmyung Women's University, directed by [Prof. Seungjun Shin](/professor/). 

Our lab focuses on bridging the gap between foundational Artificial Intelligence models and practical, real-world systems. We aim to design intelligent systems that are intrinsically optimized for AI workloads, ensuring high efficiency, scalability, and robust performance across various platforms.

---

## 🔬 Our Research Areas

At ANSL, we actively conduct research at the intersection of deep learning algorithms and system optimization. Our core research themes include:

* **Efficient AI (On-Device AI)**
    * We focus on making massive AI models—such as Large Language Models (LLMs) and Video Language Models (VLMs)—lightweight and efficient enough to run on resource-constrained edge environments like mobile NPUs.

* **AI for Smart Manufacturing**
    * We apply advanced computer vision and deep learning techniques to industrial environments to automate and optimize manufacturing processes, ensuring robust vision systems for quality control in complex production lines.

* **AI-Native Communications**
    * We explore the next generation of communication systems where AI is embedded at the core. This involves optimizing data transmission, network efficiency, and end-to-end neural codecs using deep learning.
